Vegetarian Food Restaurants

01 -Taste of India

Taste of India is proving its mettle when it comes to vegetarian food. This restaurant time and again marks the highest rating of satisfaction by the Vegetarian food lovers around the city. TOI has almost all the predominant Vegetarian Cuisine of India in its menu which consists South Indian, North Indian, Chatpati Chaats and drinks. The factor which adds up to its popularity is serving of hygienic food at a low price. To them purity and delectability of the dishes is paramount. One of the famous palatable dishes in the basket of their offerings is Jalfrezi and piquant pineapple raita. It has been in top preferences of the vegetarian food lovers around the city and is jam-packed most of the time..

Address:
L-1, Radhakrishna Shopping Complex Behind Sangam Cinema, Zone-II, M.P. Nagar
Bhopal – 462011 (Madhya Pradesh).

02 -Manohar Dairy and Restaurant

Manohar Dairy is the prime and accomplished eatery when it comes to sweets. But the reason for its second spot in the top 20 tally is the restaurant incorporated with the dairy. Manohar Dairy and Restaurants provides with the extravagant pure vegetarian dishes such as scrumptious chats, delicious Chola Bhaturas, tangy Pav Bhaji and much more. Furthermore, the restaurant serves Chinese and Italian cuisine with piquant shakes, sodas, and sundaes. Having a great meal followed by zesty dessert marks the versatility of this eatery.

Address:

03 - Bapu ki Kutia

If one wants to enjoy the traditional Thali eating experience, this is the best place to visit. This chain has two restaurants around the city serving delicious vegetarian food at a reasonable price. Though this restaurant is without any luxury but the ambiance it projects is hygienic and enjoyable with happy and satisfied customers. The dishes which the customers like the most are Dum aaloo, Kothe and thali. Address: – 123, Jyoti Complex, Behind Jyothi Talkies, Zone-I, MP Nagar, Bhopal (M.P.), Contact no.:0755 427 377. Roashan pura Square, T T Nagar, Beside Rajbhavan Road, Bhopal (M.P.), Phone- 0755 422 0849.

04 -Padharo Sa

This pure veg, roof top restaurant offers a surreal exposure to the traditional Marwari style of food amidst the augmenting trend in the demand for fast food. As the phrase suggests, the restaurant greets its customers by offering authentic, aperitive vegetarian food. Dal Bafla Churma thali, Beda puri Thali are its specialties in Marwari food. On the other hand, it also serves crispy moong dal mongode, Chaats, desserts and much more. The ambiance of the restaurant is pleasant, enjoyable with friendly service. Address: Roof-top, Block-A, Mansarovar Complex, Opp. Habibganj Rly. Station, Bhopal -16. Telephone: +91-755-4289454.

05 - The Urban Socialite

This restaurant of Hotel Lake View has been appreciated for its mesmerizing atmosphere and scenic privileges it provides to its customer. It offers vegetarian food but it is not up to the level of excellence, it is mediocre and also at a high cost. ThUS attracts the youth often and is a chill out spot, but at a price heavy on the pockets of the customer. The service is nice and friendly. Overall, you would have a good experience but if you are a gourmet, you would have some trouble with the food. Address: Hotel Ashoka, Lake View Road, Shymala Hills, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh 462002.

06 - Sagar Gaire

Sagar Gaire started from the bottom and touched the heights. This place is famous for its delicious Indian food for a price affordable by our student community residing in the city. Its Thali is most famous in Bhopal, and you will always get a huge rush in all its chain whenever you go for a visit. Fight for food!
