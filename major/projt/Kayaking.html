Kayaking

Places for Kayaking near Bhopal

01 - Upper Lake:

Upper Lake has been the go-to getaway in Bhopal and continues to attract visitors from cities and surrounding towns. The Lake gives an immense space for a wide array of water sports. The assistance staff is always there if you need any help. Visit the place for a picnic day with your loved ones.

Distance From Bhopal: 11.6 Km.
Location: Near CM House, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

02 - Halali Dam:

Halali Dam is another great option to kayak near Bhopal. The huge reservoir is just a great addition to the alluring charm of the place. You can enjoy mouth-watering delicacies afterwards at the MP Tourism Hotel.

Distance from Bhopal: 41.6 Km.
Location: Raisen District of Madhya Pradesh.

03 - Kaliasot Dam:

Kaliasot gives a thrilling kayaking experience which you won't forget for a long time. The drive to the place is smooth and the sight of river banks adds to the excitement. The place has several relaxation points during the trip. Visitors can enjoy kayaking as well as enjoy the sight of the charming natural enveloping the place.

Distance From Bhopal: 20.3 Km.
Location: Kolar Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
