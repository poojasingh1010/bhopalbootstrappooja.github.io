Boating

Places for Boating near Bhopal

01 - Upper Lake:

The chance of going full throttle on the waters of the biggest lake around Bhopal is too lucrative to miss. You can hire a speedboat here or go on a cruise on a beautiful evening and enjoy some water sports around Bhopal. The place also has a coffee house and MP tourism resort to relax afterwards.

Distance From Bhopal: 11.6 Km.
Location: Near CM House, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

02 - Halali Dam:

Speedboats are also available at Halali Dam for a great water thrill with friends and family. The place is secluded and provides for a great escape to enjoy some quality time. The cold breeze that blows in the evening will soothe your body and soul.

Distance from Bhopal: 41.6 Km.
Location: Raisen District of Madhya Pradesh.

03 - Kaliasot Dam:

Kaliasot Dam acts as a great location to enjoy a range of water sports, especially speed boating which is trending around the region. The magnetic charm of the place is enough to attract adventure enthusiast from all over the place.

Distance From Bhopal: 20.3 Km.
Location: Kolar Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
