Cycling in Bhopal

Satpura Cycling: Multi Adventure Edition:

If adventure is what you seek, this cycling tour through the range of Satpuras is your best bet at experiencing the ultimate combination of the best of adrenaline rushes. - Cycle through the forests and hills of Satpura, and cover the highest and deepest points in the state as you ride on the uphill and downhill terrains. - Start from Bhopal, move to Madhai via Tawa, and then onto Pachmarhi where you will be hitting the highest point in the state. - From here, the cycles will be heading downhill as you leave for Tamia Village of Patalkot, making the lowest point in the state. - At the end of this approximately 300 km of cycling experience, you will be brought back to Bhopal, along with your cycles to bring the experience to an end.
