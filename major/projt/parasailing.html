Parasailing

Places for Parasailing near Bhopal

01 - Upper Lake:

You can enjoy parasailing in Bhojtal where you can fly high while enjoying the beautiful bird's eye view of the flora and greenery of the nearby Van Vihar National Park along with the agricultural fields and human settlements. As the lake is spread across 31 sq. km, the sights are endless and ideal for photography.

Distance From Bhopal: 11.6 Km.
Location: Near CM House, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

02 - Halali Dam:

You can enjoy Parasailing over the pristine waters of Halali River, which is also a tributary of Betwa. The place has a serene feel which also makes it a good choice for family outings. Parasailing is relatively new but a growing activity in Bhopal especially around the reservoir region and the speedboats with kites soaring in the sky makes for great water sports experience around Bhopal.

Distance from Bhopal: 41.6 Km.
Location: Raisen District of Madhya Pradesh.

03 - Kaliasot Dam:

Kaliasot Dam offers an amazing parasailing experience. The thrill of the activity coupled with the picturesque views makes it a perfect spot for the adventure in Bhopal. Views of the nearby jungles along with a small Island embedded in between the river body can be enjoyed while parasailing here.

Distance From Bhopal: 20.3 Km.
Location: Kolar Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
