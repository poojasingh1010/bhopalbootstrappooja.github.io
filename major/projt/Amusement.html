Amusement And Waterparks in Bhopal

01 - Kanha Fun City Park

Well famed as the best amusement and water park in Bhopal, Kanha Fun City has numerous different venues that can be used for wedding receptions, theme parties, school picnics, and corporate parties. Both, water park and the amusement park have their own charm, and the fun-filled entertainment it offers remains unequalled by any other park. 

It is one of the most famous places to visit in Bhopal, and should definitely grace your Bhopal itinerary!

Timings: The theme park is open to visitors from 10:00 AM to 07:00 PM every day.

Entry Fee: The entry fees for adult and children is INR 300 per person.

Location: Kanha Fun City, Hoshangabad Road, Ratanpur, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh 462026

Distance from Bhopal Bus Stand: The fun park sits at a distance of about 19 km from the Bhopal Bus Stand.

02 -Crescent Water Park

Located 32 km from the city, this is considered to be the best among Water Parks in Bhopal. The park is connected by a road and hardly takes an hour to reach.

There are various slides to keep you busy the entire day, like the Reverse Slide, Pendulum Slide, Multiline Slide, etc. There is also a roller coaster in the amusement park but is restricted to people above the age of 40.

The Infinity Pool in this park is the largest one in central India and is a huge reason for many people to visit the place. The park has a special section for kids and has a very shallow pool for them to enjoy.

The rides in the amusement park cost are free for a single round and can be ridden again at a cost of INR 20.

Highlights: Infinity pool, Waterslides, Restaurant, Columbus ride.

Location: Alahada Khedi, Indore-Bhopal Bypass Road, Sehore, Madhya Pradesh.

Timings: 10 AM - 7 PM (Water Park) and 11 AM - 8 PM (Amusement Park).

Price per Person: INR 350, it includes a free rental costume. You can also purchase a fresh one if you don’t feel like renting one. A towel and a locker cost extra, INR 50 each.

03 -Peoples Mall Water Park

The People’s Mall Water Park is an amusement and water park inside a shopping mall! The thing that distinguishes this place is the life-size replicas of popular monuments around the world like the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, Eiffel Tower and many more.

If you get exhausted from the rides then just run to the eating joints at this place, some of the best eateries in Bhopal have opened an outlet here. Watch an amazing action-packed movie in the multiplex of the mall and dive into the pool of the park to channel the adrenaline built up in the body. 

The water park is of decent size and has various high slides. The place is perfect for those who seek an escape from the daily routine and want a nice getaway. 

Highlights: Children’s Gaming center, Life-size installations.

Location: Ayodhya Bypass Road, People's Campus, People's group, Bhanpur, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

Timings: 9 AM – 9 PM.

Price per Person: INR 350 exclusive of all other rides or installations.
