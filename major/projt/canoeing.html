Canoeing

Places for Canoeing near Bhopal

01 - Upper Lake:

The vast water body, that is Upper Lake, allows a good space for canoeing here, and you can also soak in the beauty of sights while enjoying the activity.

Distance From Bhopal: 11.6 Km.
Location: Near CM House, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

02 - Halali Dam:

A great picnic spot in the vicinity of Bhopal city, Halali Dam is a great choice for canoeing, owing to its waters apt for the sport. The place is surrounded by the scenic beauty of nature and makes for a great getaway. It is also a perfect place for nature and landscape photography and can be visited with family and friends.

Distance from Bhopal: 41.6 Km.
Location: Raisen District of Madhya Pradesh.

03 - Kaliasot Dam:

The River Dam of Kaliasot is a great option for enjoying the activity of canoeing. The place is always thronged with visitors and adrenaline junkies, who are looking to indulge in water sports near Bhopal. It also offers a great trail for a walk along the shores of the river.

Distance From Bhopal: 20.3 Km.
Location: Kolar Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
