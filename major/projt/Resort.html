Resorts in Bhopal

01 - Touchwood Resort

If you wish to experience the unmatched hospitality of Central India, this is the right place, to begin with. Touchwood Resort offers a unique combination of elegance and hospitality which is indeed a rare combination.

One can choose from their range of suites and deluxe rooms which also flaunt the panoramic balcony view. And this is not an end to the list of their specialties. The lip-smacking cuisine served by them will leave you in an awe of their food taste. With its luxurious suites and high-quality service, it is listed among the best resorts near Bhopal.

Amenities: From Lakeside dining and doctor-on-call to parking and room service, the resort offers everything that makes it one of the most popular resorts in Bhopal. High-speed internet facility is also available.

Activities: The opulent resort features a restaurant and a bar lounge where you can spend your leisurely hours.

Price: The resort charges around 4000 INR per night for two guests.

Location: The resort is situated in Mendora village in Bhopal.

02 - Jehan Numa Palace

Rated as the best resorts in Bhopal, this property serves you with numerous facilities that will make your stay worthwhile. Jehan Numa Palace is just 1 Km away from the Van Vihar National Park and Museum Of Man.

The hotel is 6 Km from Bhopal Junction Railway Station and 14 Km from Raj Bhoj Domestic Airport. The palace is also known for the variety of food that they serve to their guests. From Italian cuisines to Mughlai specialty food, one can have it all at this resort.

Amenities: The palace features amenities such as free WiFi, airport shuttle, swimming pool, fitness center, and bar. In addition to this, you can also try different dining options at their restaurants named La Kuchina, Cafe Chino, Shahnama and Under The Mango Tree.

Activities: Spend some time inside the swimming pool or sweat out at their fitness center. The resort also features a massage parlor and a wellness center where guests can relax for hours.

Price: The price for a day stay in this resort is around 4000 INR.

Location: The resort falls on Shamala Hills in Bhopal.

03 - Clark Resort

Clark Resort is a decent resort in Bhopal which features well-furnished rooms complete with private bathrooms and seating area. The guests can also enjoy the continental breakfast at this property.

Their on-site restaurant serves delicious Indian cuisines which will delight your taste buds. Clarks Resort is located at the distance of 34 Km from Van Vihar National Park and 43 Km from People’s Mall.

Amenities: With free WiFi, a fitness center, swimming pool, daily maid service, and VIP room facilities the resort has everything that one can ask for.

Activities: Delve into the world of water sports or spend some time with your kids in the Game room. The property also flaunts an indoor play area and tennis room where you can enhance your gaming skills.

Price: Standard tariff for the stay in this resort is INR 5000.

Location: The resort falls on Kheri-Bhopal road.

04 - Wood Inn Resort

Perched at a distance of 7 Km from the popular People’s Mall, this resort offers a bountiful of facilities including a swimming pool, a garden, and air-conditioned rooms.

The property lies in close proximity to several tourist spots such as Museum Of Man and Van Vihar National Park. The resort is situated at the distance of merely 2 Km from Raj Bhoj Domestic Airport.

Wood Inn Resort is rated among the best resorts in Bhopal and it serves the customers at very reasonable rates.

Amenities: The amenities provided by this resort cover free parking, airport shuttle, Daily maid service, BBQ facilities, and Ironing service. The resort also features VIP room and meeting facilities for their special guests.

Activities: From playing table tennis to swimming in the outdoor pool, guests can enjoy a wide range of activities here. Since the resort also features a restaurant, you can also enjoy some local cuisines of the city.

Price: The price for one day stay in Wood Inn Resort is around 3500 INR.

Location: Wood Inn Resort is situated on Airport Road, opposite the Lake Pearl Colony.

05 -Caravan resort

Boasting a huge garden and 24-hour front desk, this resort welcomes its guests with a huge outdoor pool and a restaurant. Each unit at this resort is well-equipped with a flat-screen TV and a seating area.

Guests enjoy their daily buffet breakfast which comprises a variety of cuisines. The nearest airport to this property is Raja Bhoj Domestic Airport.

Amenities: The resort offers umpteen facilities including free WiFi, 24-hour front desk, laundry, photocopying, and an outdoor pool.

Activities: If you are a fitness freak, spend some time at their fitness center. The resort also features a swimming pool where one can relax after a hectic day.

Price: The accommodation price for this resort falls in the range of 2000-3000 INR.

Location: Caravan resort falls on Ratibad Bhadbhada Road in Bhopal.

06 -Courtyard by Marriott

Featuring a huge pool, Marriott flaunts well-furnished rooms with beautifully designed glass windows and 24-hour room service. The resort also has 2 bars and 2 restaurants that offer 24-hour in-room dining.

The restaurants serve their guests with Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines which suit the taste of people from all the regions. This is the most luxurious resort in Bhopal which lies at the distance of 7 Km from Bhopal Railway Station.

Amenities: The resort provides the facilities such as free WiFi, airport shuttle, free parking, car rental, and dry cleaning services. Apart from this, the hotel authorities also help their guests in making the necessary travel arrangements.

Activities: Begin your day at their fitness center or spend your evening in the in-house bar, the property has it all to keep its guests delighted.

Price: The standard price for the stay in this resort is INR 8000.

Location: The resort is situated in DB City, Arera Hills, Bhopal.

07 -Heritage Noor-Us-Sabah Palace

Reckoned among the decent resorts in Bhopal, WelcomHeritage Noor-Us-Sabah Palace has enough amenities to keep their guests elated. The guests can kick-start their day by working out in the fitness center or spend a laidback time watching entertainment shows on the satellite TV.

The palace is just a 13 -minute drive away from Raja Bhoj Domestic Airport. Its proximity to the tourist spots such as Van Vihar National Park and Museum Of Man makes it the most sought-after resort in Bhopal.

Amenities: With free parking, free internet, and room service, the resort provides all the basic services that one can expect. Apart from the basic ones, you can also enjoy their in-house restaurant service which serves the guests with Indian, Continental, Chinese, and Italian food.

Activities: Spend some peaceful time at their wellness and spa center or revive your childhood with your small kids in the children’s playground. One can also plan an eventful night by availing their barbecue facility.

Price: The standard price for accommodation in this resort is around INR 6000.

Location: The resort is situated on VIP Road, near Koh-E-Fiza, Bhopal.

08 -Samardha Jungle Resort

If you are looking for a peaceful accommodation, booking your stay in this resort may do the trick. Samardha Jungle Resort is situated at the distance of 15 Km from Kanha Fun City and 20 Km from Museum Of Man.

The resort offers a 24-hour front desk service and tour arrangements for the guests. The entire unit is well-equipped and well-maintained to suit the needs of the guests.

Amenities: Along with the free parking and room service, the resort also provides a wake-up service. All the rooms feature a garden view, flat screen TV, and private bathroom.

Activities: Enjoy the garden view from the balcony or plan a day-out for shopping as the surroundings of the resort is considered as the best place to shop.

Price: The price for accommodation in this resort is around INR 3000.

Location: The resort is situated on Samardha forest road in the village Padaria.
