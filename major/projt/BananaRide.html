Banana Ride

Places for Banana Ride near Bhopal

01 - Upper Lake:

Upper Lake is one such place where you can enjoy banana rides. You can indulge in a group ride, carrying up to 12 people, and enjoy a visit to nearby Kamla Park. The spot is quite popular among tourists and is rapidly becoming a haven for thrill seekers and people interested in watersports.

Distance From Bhopal: 11.6 Km.
Location: Near CM House, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

02 - Halali Dam:

Situated in the outskirts of the main city centre, this dam is a great getaway for thrill seekers. One can enjoy banana boat rides on a romantic outing or a getaway with friends. The calm atmosphere is perfect for a family picnic outing and catching some jaw-dropping spectacles of the gushing water from the reservoir.

Distance from Bhopal: 41.6 Km.
Location: Raisen District of Madhya Pradesh.

03 - Kaliasot Dam:

Kaliasot provides a great ambience and setting for adventure lovers. The place offers calm and soothing waters and a bird-eye view of the city. You can enjoy a banana ride here. The nearby jungle around the area is also very alluring and is worth exploring. Since it's not very far from the city, it is frequented by tourists and enthusiasts looking for water sports around Bhopal.

Distance From Bhopal: 20.3 Km.
Location: Kolar Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
