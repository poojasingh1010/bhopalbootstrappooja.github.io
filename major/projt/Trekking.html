01 Experience Camping Under Mahua Trees:

Bhopal is replete with many gems of hidden spots that are yet to be found and explored. Be it the lakes of the city or the forests that surround the city. One such region of amazing nature is the jungle of Samardha, where this package of adventure camping experience awaits.

Factual information:

This place was once the hunting ground for Royal Nawabs of Bhopal. The villagers of Samardha, with their love for nature, are the people who run this place. There is a highway road surrounded by clusters of natural flora and lush greenery all around taking you towards the Jungle Camp. Another attraction of the Jungle camp is exposure to the culture of very small Samardha village as this jungle camp is the main source of livelihood for Samardha villagers. This region of the jungle is famous for the trees of Mahua, that are used in making local wine.

Stay:

Comfortable Camping Tents on double/triple sharing basis, that come along with constructed washrooms. The Jungle camp has a camping capacity of 25 people.

Activity:

The highlight of this the experience is the amazing trekking trail that leads you into the jungles of Samardha, where nature and wildlife prevail at their best. Also, the region is a hotspot for many species of birds and makes for a good experience of birdwatching. Apart from these, you can indulge in many rope courses, Chess, carom, billiards, Basketball, badminton, cycling, yoga,

How To Reach:

This campsite is located in the jungles of Samardha, 15 km away from Bhopal. From Bhopal take the Raisen road till village Padariya which is a forest checkpoint, from where the campsite is not much far away

03 - Trek To Ginnorgarh Fort:

Bhopal, popularly known as the city of lakes, is also a city of unmatched natural beauty. Dotted with many natural wonders, there are many manmade wonders that have also found a place in the history of this city, and one of them is the majestic fort of Ginnorgarh.

Factual Information:

Ginnorgarh is a historical fort, situated 61 kms away from Bhopal and 3 km from Delawadi. Standing on a 1127 meters long and 266 meters broad isolated hill, the fort is a trekkers paradise, surrounded by picturesque location and famous for Parrots. Earlier Ginnorgarh fort was under the stronghold of the Gonds King, but later it fell to Dost Mohammad, the ruler of Bhopal State. The fort is accessible only on foot from Delawadi.

Activity:

The trek leading to the fort is an amazing and picturesque one, as you climb to reach the fort located on the top of this isolated hill. The fort which is cut from a rock is a sight to behold and is the highlight of this trek. From here, you get panoramic views of the area of Delawadi and beyond.

How to reach?

Located approximately 60 kms South of Bhopal, the fort is located in the midst of the Delawadi Range. Road transport will bring you directly to Delawadi, from where the fort is accessible only by foot. You will have to trek uphill for about a couple of hours through a dense jungle to finally arrive at the Fort. This package includes a road transfer from and back to Bhopal.

04 - Forsyth Trail:

One of the unexplored and untouched locations situated in the plateaus of Panchmari “the land of the five hills” welcomes you to the Forsyth Trail. An adventure expedition takes you back in time and into the wild ranges of Satpura also which retraces the footsteps of Capt. James Forsyth and walk the path he took over 150 years ago to discover Pachmarhi.

Factual Information:

Over 150 years ago, Captain James Forsyth’s expedition for land settlements for British India led to the plateau’s discovery. While in Satpura, he took a route through the core tiger zone. Today, this path is known as the ‘Forsyth Trail’, and is commonly retraced by wildlife enthusiasts who wish to experience the forest intimately. This is a circular trail which involves crossing a couple of streams and the magnificent Denwa Backwaters. As you trek along, you will also come across ‘Bharias’ – tribes indigenous to the valleys, known for their ancient art of healing. The hike through the middle of a tiger reserve and surrounded by lush Deciduous Jungles of Satpura and the unexplored, untouched and unbounded territory will change the way you experience wildlife.

Activity:

The trek itself is a beautiful experience, followed by much more surreal views at the end of it. Amidst all the natural beauty and is worth capturing in your memories, as well as in your photographs. Apart from these, the other grand highlight is an interaction with the indigenous tribe known as the ‘Bharias’. A local forest guide and our naturalist help you spot herbivores like Gaur, Chital, Nilgai and Sambar, Owls, Wild Cats, Bears, Civets, etc. If you’re lucky, you may meet the world’s smallest wildcat – the Rusty-spotted Cat. The way passes lush through lush green jungles which are home to many wild animals and rare birds. Camping alongside Denwa backwaters in the night provides an unforgettable experience.

How to Reach?

Located around 120 km from the city of Bhopal and in the midst of the Satpura Wildlife Sanctuary, the place can be reached by taking a road transport to the nearest village of Pipariya. From here, you can trek to the Forsyth Trail, which is about a couple of hours away. This package includes a pick and drop facility from Bhopal, and back to Bhopal.

06 - Trek to Salamatpur:

Bhopal is a city that is always bustling with life all throughout the year, but the most amazing experiences in this city all lie on the outskirts of this place. One such region worth seeing is the region of Salamatpur, and this trek takes you there in the most adventurous way possible; by trekking.

Factual Information:

Known for the early finding settlers finding their home here, Salamatpur is a great spot for trekkers who love the beauty of nature. Surrounded by a good number of cave shelters in the hills, the highlight of visiting Salamatpur are the beautiful peacocks and many other rare migratory birds flocking to this region throughout the year. A trek here opens one up to some of the most beautiful scenes of the wild and rare bird sightings.

Activity:

The main activity of this adventure is the trekking trail itself, where you would witness the rush of walking through the jungle and enjoy the sighting of many rare migratory species. Another great thing to do on this trek is visiting the ancient rock shelters where you can find a lot of evidence of the early settlements.

How to Reach:

Located almost 40 km north-east of the city of Bhopal, a road transport will directly take you to the starting of the Salamatpur region. The trek to reach the cave shelters starts from the Salamatpur dam. In this package, you will be picked up from Bhopal and transferred via road to the beginning point of the trek.

07 - Saru Maru Caves:

Bhopal, the city of lakes, also happens to be a destination rich in traditional and cultural history. Lined with a number of attractions that date back centuries and have a story to tell each, Bhopal is also the home to the famous Saru Maru Caves. This trek takes you to these cave formations which also have a waterfall nearby them. Trek deep into the heart of the Satpura, get drenched in the serene waterfall and live life to the fullest.

Factual information:

Saru Maru Caves are a famous attraction featuring the archaeological site of an ancient monastic complex and Buddhist caves. The site contains a number of stupas as well as natural caves for monks. In the caves, many Buddhist graffitis have been found along with two inscriptions of Ashoka the Great in Brahmi script carved in stone 2200 years ago when he visited this site himself. Surrounded by a dense jungle and a diverse wildlife enriched location, the place is home to various rare migratory birds, all of which combined makes it a true trekkers paradise.

Activity:

The trek that passes through the dense forests to reach the cave formations of Saru Maru is scenic in all manners and treats you to vistas that are surreal. The greenery and the wildlife that welcomes you here is breathtaking, and the presence of the many rare migratory birds just adds its own charm to the whole setting. You will be seeing the amazing ancient stupas and the serene waterfall near the caves that are worth capturing in your pictures.

How to Reach?

The caves are located near the small village of Pangoraria in the Sehore district of Madhya Pradesh. From the village itself, the caves are located at a distance of 6 kms by foot, and then the waterfall from there is located some 10 kms from the Saru Maru Caves. This package includes transport to and from Bhopal for the trek.

08 - Mahadev Paani waterfall Trek:

The Ratapani Wildlife Sanctuary near Bhopal is bustling with the beauty of the wilderness and is one of the best places to find tranquility in a place away from the hectic rush of the city life. And in the Ratapani forests lies the majestic beauty of the Mahadev Paani Waterfall.

Factual Information:

Flocked by a number of migratory birds arriving here in different seasons, the area around the Mahadev Paani waterfall is one of the perfect fits for an adventurous trekking. With a natural pool forming at the waterfall, the trek in itself brings you close to the Mahadev himself, the Hindu God Lord Shiva. On the way to the falls, one can find a number of caves on the sides of the trail with a number of idols placed dedicated to Shiva.

Activity:

The 14 km long trek is the highlight of this experience as you walk through the dense jungle, all the while enjoying some of the most beautiful views of the forests and hills around the Mahadev Paani region. The guide on the trek will give you snippets of information about the Flora and Fauna indigenous to the region, making this an extensive learning experience as well. Reaching the falls will not only reward you with a refreshing sight of the waterfall but also with a chance of swimming and dipping in the cool waters of it.

How to Reach:

Located 30 km east of the city of Bhopal, Mahadev Paani waterfall can directly be reached by taking a road transport. From the top of the falls, you then have to trek down the hill to reach the viewpoint and the base of the pool. This package provides you with a road transport from a pickup point in Bhopal and brings you to Mahadev Paani
